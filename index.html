What is TAP-TAP UNICORN?

TAP-TAP UNICORN is an incredibly addictive idle clicker game where you tap the magical unicorn to earn Magic Points. Use your points to purchase upgrades that automatically generate more points for you.

The game features beautiful rainbow visuals, satisfying animations, and a progressive system that will keep you coming back for more. How many Magic Points can you collect?
